Edited Photo 1 (1920x1080)

I chose this photo because I really enjoy using sunlight as a natural filter. I love how the scenery looks when it is lit by the sun. When I first took this picture, it was slightly overexposed, so I adjusted the brightness to make it less harsh. By lowering the exposure, I was able to balance the light and make the photo look more natural, without being too bright.
